## Core concepts

Five concepts give you a complete mental model of Outpulse.

### Monitor

A monitor is a saved discovery rule. It defines *where* to look for people
on X — either the followers/following of one or more target accounts, or
the repliers to a specific tweet — and applies bio keyword and follower
filters. Monitors don't send anything; they produce a pool of profiles.

→ [Create and manage monitors](/core/monitors)

### Lead

A lead is a profile (or post) that a monitor discovered. Every lead gets
an AI fit score (1–10) based on your ICP. You can browse, filter, and
export leads from the **Leads** page.

→ [Leads](/core/leads)

### Campaign

A campaign is how you actually message people. You pick monitors as
sources, choose an AI score threshold, import the leads, then bulk-generate
DM drafts. When you're ready, click Send — Outpulse paces the batch and
delivers each DM within your daily caps.

→ [Campaigns](/core/campaigns)

### Outbound tracker

Every sent DM appears in the **Outbound Tracker** (the **Inbox** tab) with
a manual pipeline status. Outpulse doesn't poll X for inbound replies —
you handle conversations natively on X via a one-click deep link.

→ [Outbound tracker](/core/inbox)

### Credit

Credits are the unit of usage. The two billable actions are:

- **AI draft generation** — 0.25 credits per draft
- **DM send** — 5 credits per successful delivery

Scanning, scoring, and filtering are all free. Your monthly plan refills
your credit bucket each billing cycle, and top-up packs add rollover
credits that never expire.

→ [How credits work](/credits/how-credits-work)

## The sending loop

```
Monitor (continuously)
   ├─ Scan followers / following / tweet repliers
   ├─ Filter by bio keywords and follower range
   ├─ AI fit-score each profile (1–10)
   └─ Save as Lead

You build a Campaign
   ├─ Pick monitors as sources
   ├─ Choose AI score threshold
   └─ Import leads (dedupes against other campaigns)

You generate drafts
   ├─ Bulk: every pending lead at once
   └─ Per-lead: regenerate or edit individually
       — 0.25 credits per draft

You click Send
   ├─ Each lead enqueued with a human-like scheduledAt
   ├─ Per-workspace queue worker processes one at a time
   ├─ Schedule + daily cap enforced on every send
   └─ DM delivered via X API v2
       — 5 credits per successful send
```

## Account safety

Outpulse is built to protect your X account from being flagged.

- **Human-like delays** between every send — a 50-DM batch is spread
  across hours, not seconds
- **Daily caps enforced automatically** per connected account — 30 DMs
  per day on standard accounts, 15 during warm-up
- **Warm-up mode** for new or small accounts (under 50 followers, first
  14 days) — caps drop to 15 DMs per day, then return to standard once
  the account ages out
- **Schedule windows** — sends only happen inside your configured hours
  and days
- **Failed-send protection** — DMs that hit a cap or land outside your
  schedule are deferred, not failed

These safeguards run automatically. You don't need to configure them.

<Note>
  **A note on platform compliance.** Outpulse is built for targeted
  outreach — not mass messaging. Keep your bio keyword filters tight,
  start with AI-curated imports at threshold 7+, and review the first 50
  DMs you send before scaling. Reading X's
  [Automation Rules](https://help.twitter.com/en/rules-and-policies/x-automation)
  is worth your time.
</Note>
