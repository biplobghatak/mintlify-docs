This guide walks you through the full onboarding flow from signup to your first signal in the inbox.

## 1. Create your account

Go to [outpulse.app/signup](https://outpulse.app/signup). You can sign up with:

- **X account** (recommended — connects your X account in the same step)
- **Google account**
- **Email and password**

<Note>
  If you sign up with email, check your inbox for a verification link before continuing. Google and X signups skip this step.
</Note>

After verifying, you'll land on the onboarding flow.

## 2. Name your workspace

Your workspace groups your monitors, signals, drafts, and settings together. The default name is "My Workspace" — rename it to match your product or brand.

You can create additional workspaces later for separate products or X accounts.

## 3. Connect your X account

Outpulse needs access to your X account to post replies and send DMs on your behalf.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Connect X account'">
    You'll be redirected to X's OAuth authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Outpulse">
    Grant the requested permissions. Outpulse requests read and write access to post on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to onboarding">
    You'll be redirected back automatically. Your account name and avatar will appear as confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can connect multiple X accounts later from **Settings → Connected accounts**. Each account can be assigned to a workspace independently.
</Info>

## 4. Set up your ICP

Your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) tells Outpulse who you're trying to reach. This powers the intent scoring engine.

Fill in at minimum:

- **Product name and description** — what your product does and who it's for
- **Target job titles** — e.g. "Head of Marketing", "Founder", "Growth lead"
- **Pain points** — problems your product solves
- **Keywords** — terms your ICP would use when they have the problem you solve

The more specific you are, the more accurate the scoring. You can update this at any time from **Settings → ICP**.

→ [Full ICP setup guide](/core/icp)

## 5. Set up your tone profile

Paste 3–5 of your own posts from X. Outpulse uses these to learn your writing style — sentence length, tone, vocabulary, emoji usage.

<Tip>
  Use posts where you're engaging with someone, not just broadcasting. Conversational posts train the AI better than announcements.
</Tip>

→ [Full tone profile guide](/core/tone-profile)

## 6. Create your first monitor

A monitor is a saved search that Outpulse runs every 20 minutes. You can set up:

- **Keyword monitor** — e.g. `"looking for CRM tool"` or `"recommend project management software"`
- **Hashtag monitor** — e.g. `#buildinpublic`
- **Competitor account** — monitor replies to a competitor's posts
- **Account monitor** — watch a specific account for new posts

Set the **intent threshold** to 7 (default). Posts scored below this won't appear in your inbox.

→ [Full monitors guide](/core/monitors)

## 7. Wait for your first signal

Outpulse runs its first scan within 20 minutes of your monitor being created. Head to the **Inbox** to see results.

Each signal shows:
- The original post
- The author's profile and follower count
- The intent score (1–10) and reason for the ICP match
- Action buttons: generate draft, skip, or mark outcome

→ [How to work the inbox](/core/inbox)

## You're set up

From here, your typical daily flow is:

1. Check the inbox for new signals
2. Generate and approve drafts
3. Review the queue before posts go out
4. Track people who respond in the People tab
