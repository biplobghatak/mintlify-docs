Outpulse connects to Bluesky using an **app password** — a scoped credential you generate in Bluesky that grants posting and DM access without exposing your real password. Tokens are encrypted with AES-256 at rest.

<Note>
  Bluesky does not support third-party OAuth the same way X does, so app passwords are the official way for external tools to post on your behalf. You can revoke an app password at any time from Bluesky settings.
</Note>

## Create an app password in Bluesky

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Bluesky settings">
    Go to [bsky.app/settings/app-passwords](https://bsky.app/settings/app-passwords) while signed in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new app password">
    Click **Add App Password**, give it a recognizable name like `Outpulse`, and make sure the option to **Allow access to direct messages** is enabled — Outpulse needs it to send DMs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the generated password">
    Bluesky shows the password only once, in the format `xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx`. Copy it before closing the dialog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect the account in Outpulse

1. Go to **Settings → Connected accounts**
2. In the **Bluesky** card, click **Connect Bluesky**
3. Enter your Bluesky **handle** (e.g. `yourname.bsky.social` or your custom domain) and paste the **app password**
4. Click **Connect**

Outpulse validates the credentials, stores the resulting session tokens encrypted, and fetches your profile info.

<Warning>
  Never paste your main Bluesky password. Outpulse will reject it, and even if it succeeded it would expose your full account. Always use an app password with a name you recognize so you can revoke it cleanly later.
</Warning>

## What Outpulse can do on Bluesky

| Capability | How it's used |
|------------|---------------|
| Read posts via AppView search | Monitors scan for keywords, hashtags, and posts from tracked accounts |
| Fetch profiles | Display follower counts and profile context on signals |
| Post replies | Send the public ping reply in Reply + DM mode |
| Send chat messages | Deliver the DM through Bluesky's chat (DM) API |

Outpulse does not read your chat inbox history and does not browse your follows or followers list.

## Character limit

Bluesky posts are capped at **300 graphemes** (not UTF-16 code units). Outpulse counts graphemes when generating drafts and will refuse to send a reply or DM that exceeds the cap.

## Pausing an account

Pausing stops all posting from the Bluesky account without disconnecting it. Monitors targeting Bluesky keep scanning and scoring, but no drafts are sent until you resume.

To pause: **Settings → Connected accounts → Pause** on the Bluesky card.

## Disconnecting an account

Disconnecting removes the account from Outpulse and clears the encrypted session tokens. Any monitors using that Bluesky account are paused automatically.

To disconnect: **Settings → Connected accounts → Disconnect** on the Bluesky card.

<Warning>
  Disconnecting in Outpulse does not revoke the app password on Bluesky's side. To fully revoke access, go to [bsky.app/settings/app-passwords](https://bsky.app/settings/app-passwords) and delete the `Outpulse` app password there.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to check |
|---------|---------------|
| "Invalid credentials" | Confirm the handle is correct (no leading `@`) and the app password hasn't been revoked |
| DMs fail to send | The app password must be created with **direct messages** access enabled. Recreate it with DM access and reconnect |
| "Rate limited" on connect | Bluesky rate-limits login attempts — wait 15 minutes before retrying |
| Account reconnect needed | App password sessions can expire. Disconnect and reconnect with a fresh app password |
