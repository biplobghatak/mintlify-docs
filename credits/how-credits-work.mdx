Outpulse uses a **subscription + top-up** model. Every plan includes a monthly credit refill. If you need more mid-cycle, you can buy a top-up pack.

## What are credits?

Credits are the unit of usage in Outpulse. Every action that requires a post to X costs a fixed number of credits. Scanning, intent scoring, and AI draft generation are all free — you only pay when a DM (or DM + ping combo) is actually sent.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| **Send DM only** | 2 credits |
| **Send Reply + DM (ping combo)** | 5 credits |

## Free actions

These are always free, regardless of plan:

- Running monitors (scanning X every 20 minutes)
- Intent scoring and ICP filtering
- Generating and regenerating AI drafts
- Viewing the inbox, signals, analytics
- Filtering, sorting, and searching
- Managing pipeline stages and notes

## Two credit buckets

Your balance is split into two buckets:

| Bucket | Source | Behaviour |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Subscription credits** | Monthly plan refill | Reset to your plan's monthly allowance at the start of every billing cycle |
| **Rollover credits** | Top-up pack purchases | Never expire — carry forward indefinitely |

When Outpulse deducts credits, it drains your **subscription bucket first**, then falls back to rollover credits. This means your rollover credits act as a permanent safety net and are never wasted by a monthly reset.

## Monthly refill

At the start of each billing cycle, your subscription bucket is reset to your plan's monthly credit allowance. Any unused subscription credits from the previous month do not carry over — only rollover (top-up) credits persist.

| Plan | Monthly credits |
|------|----------------|
| Solo | 1,000 |
| Growth | 4,000 |
| Agency | 12,000 |

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance appears in the top navigation bar of the dashboard. Click it to go to the Credits page and see:

- Current balance (subscription + rollover)
- Usage breakdown by action type
- Full credit ledger (every debit and refill)

## Credits are per account, not per workspace

Your credit balance is shared across all workspaces. You don't need separate credit pools for different workspaces.

## Atomic transactions

Every credit deduction is logged atomically — if an action fails after credits are charged, you'll see a credit refund entry in your ledger. You won't be charged for failed posts.
