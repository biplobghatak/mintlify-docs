---
title: "How credits work"
description: "Outpulse uses a pay-as-you-go credit system. You only pay for the actions that use compute or API calls."
---

Outpulse doesn't have monthly subscription plans. You buy credits upfront and spend them as you use the product. Credits never expire.

## What are credits?

Credits are the unit of usage in Outpulse. Every action that requires compute or an API call costs a fixed number of credits. Browsing the inbox, filtering, viewing analytics, and managing settings are all free.

You're charged only for the valuable actions:

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Lead found (signal meets ICP and intent threshold) | 2 credits |
| Generate 1 draft (reply or DM) | 1 credit |
| Publish 1 reply or DM to X | 2 credits |
| Regenerate a rejected draft | 1 credit |

→ [Full credit cost breakdown](/credits/credit-costs)

## Scanning is free

Outpulse runs your monitors every 20 minutes — that scanning is not charged. You're only charged when a post passes your ICP and intent threshold and becomes a signal (a "lead found").

This means you can run many monitors without accumulating costs from posts that don't match your criteria.

## Credits never expire

Credits you purchase don't have an expiry date. Buy a bundle now and use it at your own pace — whether that's over two weeks or six months.

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance appears in the top navigation bar of the dashboard. Click it to go to the Credits page and see:

- Current balance
- Usage breakdown by action type
- Today's spend
- Full credit ledger (every debit and purchase)

## Credits are per account, not per workspace

Your credit balance is shared across all workspaces. You don't need separate credit pools for different workspaces.

## Atomic transactions

Every credit deduction is logged atomically — if an action fails after credits are charged, you'll see a credit refund entry in your ledger. You won't be charged for failed posts.
