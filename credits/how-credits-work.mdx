Outpulse uses a **subscription + top-up** model. Every plan includes a
monthly credit refill. If you need more mid-cycle, you can buy a top-up pack
that never expires.

## What are credits?

Credits are the unit of usage in Outpulse. Every action that hits the X API
or runs an AI draft costs a fixed number of credits. Scanning, intent
scoring, and ICP filtering are all free — you only spend credits on AI
drafts and on DMs that are actually sent.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| **Generate DM draft** | 0.25 credits |
| **Send DM** | 5 credits |
| **Send Reply + DM (ping combo)** | 5 credits |

→ [Full credit cost breakdown](/credits/credit-costs)

## Free actions

These are always free, regardless of plan:

- Running monitors (Outpulse scans X every 20 minutes)
- Intent scoring and ICP filtering
- Viewing the inbox, signals, analytics
- Filtering, sorting, and searching
- Managing pipeline stages and notes

## Two credit buckets

Your balance is split into two buckets:

| Bucket | Source | Behaviour |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Subscription credits** | Monthly plan refill | Reset to your plan's monthly allowance at the start of every billing cycle |
| **Rollover credits** | Top-up pack purchases | Never expire — carry forward indefinitely |

When Outpulse deducts credits, it drains your **subscription bucket first**,
then falls back to rollover credits. This means your rollover credits act as
a permanent safety net and are never wasted by a monthly reset.

## Monthly refill

At the start of each billing cycle, your subscription bucket is reset to
your plan's monthly credit allowance. Any unused subscription credits from
the previous month do not carry over — only rollover (top-up) credits
persist.

| Plan | Monthly credits |
|------|----------------|
| Solo | 2,000 |
| Growth | 7,000 |
| Agency | 35,000 |

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance appears in the top navigation bar of the
dashboard. Click it to go to the Credits page and see:

- Current balance (subscription + rollover)
- Usage breakdown by action type
- Full credit ledger (every debit and refill)

## Credits are per account, not per workspace

Your credit balance is shared across all workspaces. You don't need separate
credit pools for different workspaces.

## Atomic transactions

Every credit deduction is logged atomically — if an action fails after
credits are charged, you'll see a credit refund entry in your ledger. You
won't be charged for failed posts.
