title: "Credit costs"
description: "A complete breakdown of how many credits each action costs in Outpulse."
---

Every billable action in Outpulse has a fixed credit cost. Scanning,
profile filtering, and AI fit-scoring are free — you only spend credits
on AI drafts and on DMs that are actually delivered.

## Action costs

| Action | Credits | When it's charged |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Generate DM draft** | 0.25 | When an AI DM draft is successfully generated for a campaign lead |
| **Regenerate DM draft** | 0.25 | Each regeneration is a fresh AI call, charged the same as the original draft |
| **Send DM** | 5 | When a DM is successfully delivered via X |

Failed generations and failed sends do **not** consume credits. If credits
are charged and the action then fails downstream, the deduction is reversed
in your ledger automatically.

## Free actions

These actions are always free:

- Running monitors (scanning followers, following, and tweet repliers)
- Bio keyword and follower-range filtering
- AI fit scoring (1–10) on every discovered profile
- Importing leads from monitors into a campaign
- Editing a draft (typing / manual changes — no AI call)
- Re-sending a failed lead (only counted as 5 credits if the resend itself succeeds)
- Viewing campaigns, leads, the outbound tracker, and analytics
- Filtering, sorting, searching, and exporting CSV/Markdown/JSON
- Updating thread pipeline status and notes
- Changing settings

## Where the costs land

Outpulse is currently DM-only. The full lifecycle for a single prospect:

1. Monitor scans — **free**
2. Profile passes bio + follower filters — **free**
3. AI fit score assigned — **free**
4. You import the lead into a campaign — **free**
5. You generate a draft — **0.25 credits**
6. You click Send — **5 credits on successful delivery**

So the all-in cost of one fully personalized DM is **5.25 credits**, of
which 5 are only charged if the send actually goes through.
