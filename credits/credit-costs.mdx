Every billable action in Outpulse has a fixed credit cost. Here's the full breakdown.

## Action costs

| Action | Credits | When it's charged |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Lead found** | 2 | When a post passes your ICP check and intent threshold, becoming a signal in your inbox |
| **Generate draft** | 1 | When you generate a reply or DM draft for a signal |
| **Publish reply or DM** | 2 | When a post is successfully sent to X |
| **Regenerate draft** | 1 | When you regenerate a draft after rejecting the previous one |
| **Profile enrichment** | 1 | When Outpulse fetches full profile data for a contact |

## Free actions

These actions are always free:

- Running monitors (scanning X every 20 minutes)
- Viewing signals in the inbox
- Filtering, sorting, and searching
- Skipping signals
- Viewing people and analytics
- Browsing the queue
- Updating pipeline stages and notes
- Changing settings

## Estimating your monthly spend

Here's a rough estimate based on usage patterns:

### Light usage (50 signals/month)
- 50 leads found × 2 = 100 credits
- 30 drafts generated × 1 = 30 credits
- 25 posts sent × 2 = 50 credits
- **Total: ~180 credits/month**

### Moderate usage (200 signals/month)
- 200 leads found × 2 = 400 credits
- 150 drafts generated × 1 = 150 credits
- 100 posts sent × 2 = 200 credits
- **Total: ~750 credits/month**

### Heavy usage (500+ signals/month)
- 500 leads found × 2 = 1,000 credits
- 400 drafts generated × 1 = 400 credits
- 300 posts sent × 2 = 600 credits
- **Total: ~2,000 credits/month**

<Tip>
  To reduce credit spend without reducing impact: raise your intent threshold (fewer but higher-quality signals), use smart auto instead of generating drafts for every signal manually, and skip signals that clearly aren't worth pursuing.
</Tip>
