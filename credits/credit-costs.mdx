---
title: "Credit costs"
description: "A complete breakdown of how many credits each action costs in Outpulse."
---

Every billable action in Outpulse has a fixed credit cost. Scanning, intent
scoring, and ICP filtering are free — you only spend credits on AI drafts and
on DMs that are actually delivered.

## Action costs

| Action | Credits | When it's charged |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Generate DM draft** | 0.25 | When an AI DM draft is successfully generated for a campaign signal |
| **Send DM** | 5 | When a DM is successfully delivered to X |
| **Send Reply + DM (ping combo)** | 5 | When Outpulse fires a public reply ping and the DM is delivered |

Regenerating a draft costs the same 0.25 credits as the initial generation.
Failed generations and failed sends do **not** consume credits — if a charge
goes through and the action then fails, the deduction is refunded to your
ledger automatically.

## Free actions

These actions are always free:

- Running monitors (Outpulse scans X every 20 minutes)
- Intent scoring and ICP filtering
- Bio prefiltering (signal-quality check)
- Editing a draft (typing / manual changes — no AI call)
- Viewing signals, the outbound tracker, people, and analytics
- Filtering, sorting, and searching
- Updating pipeline stages and notes
- Changing settings

## Reply + DM vs DM only

Each monitor has an **outreach mode** that controls how the DM is delivered.

**Reply + DM** sends a short public reply to the prospect's post first —
"Just sent you a DM! 👋" — before delivering the personalized DM. This
triggers an X push notification that a raw DM alone often doesn't, lifting
open rates significantly. It counts as a single billable action at **5
credits**.

**DM only** skips the public ping and sends only the private DM. Use this on
monitors where you'd rather not leave a public trail. It also costs **5
credits** per send.

The outreach mode is configured per monitor on the monitor's settings page.
Both modes use the same 0.25-credit AI draft cost.
