Every billable action in Outpulse has a fixed credit cost. Here's the full breakdown.

## Action costs

| Action | Credits | When it's charged |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Send DM only** | 2 | When a DM is successfully delivered to X |
| **Send Reply + DM (ping combo)** | 5 | When Outpulse fires a public "Just sent you a DM!" ping reply and then sends the personalized DM |

## Free actions

These actions are always free:

- Running monitors (scanning X every 20 minutes)
- Intent scoring and ICP filtering
- Bio prefiltering (Step A signal quality check)
- Generating AI drafts
- Regenerating or editing drafts
- Viewing signals, the outbound tracker, people, and analytics
- Filtering, sorting, and searching
- Updating pipeline stages and notes
- Changing settings

## Reply + DM vs DM only

The **Reply + DM** combo (5 credits) sends a short public reply to the prospect's post first — "Just sent you a DM!" — before delivering the personalized DM. This triggers an X push notification that a raw DM alone often doesn't, lifting open rates significantly. It counts as a single billable action.

**DM only** (2 credits) skips the public ping and sends only the private DM. Use this on monitors where you prefer not to leave a public trail.

The outreach mode is configured per monitor.
