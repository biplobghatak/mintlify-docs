Outpulse credits come from two sources: your **monthly subscription** (which
refills automatically each billing cycle) and **top-up packs** (one-time
purchases of lifetime credits).

## Subscription plans

| Plan | Price | Monthly credits | Workspaces | Best for |
|------|-------|----------------|------------|----------|
| **Solo** | $49 / mo | 2,000 | 2 (1 X account each) | Founders & freelancers |
| **Growth** | $149 / mo | 7,000 | Up to 10 (1 X account each) | B2B teams scaling outreach |
| **Agency** | $499 / mo | 35,000 | Unlimited (1 X account each) | Agencies & high-volume teams |

All plans include:

- All monitor types and signal templates
- Free AI drafting & regeneration (0.25 credits per DM draft)
- Outbound tracker and analytics
- Free scanning, intent scoring, and ICP filtering

Subscription credits reset at the start of every billing cycle. Unused
subscription credits do not carry over — only top-up rollover credits
persist month to month.

## Top-up pack

| Pack | Credits | Price | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|-------|
| **Top-up** | 1,000 | $20 | Credits never expire — roll over forever |

Top-up credits go into your rollover bucket and are only spent after your
monthly subscription credits are exhausted. They're ideal for high-activity
months or bridging a gap between billing cycles.

## Subscribing or topping up

1. Go to **Credits** in the left sidebar
2. Choose a subscription plan or click **Buy top-up**
3. You'll be redirected to the checkout page (powered by Dodo Payments)
4. Complete payment
5. Credits are added to your balance immediately after payment confirmation

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Credits purchase page showing the plan cards and top-up option]

## Payment methods

Credit card and other payment methods are supported via Dodo Payments.
Accepted methods are shown at checkout.

## Purchase history

Your full purchase history is available in the Credits ledger. Each
subscription refill and top-up purchase appears as a positive entry with the
amount, timestamp, and type.

## Receipts

A receipt is emailed to your account email address after every successful
purchase or subscription renewal.

---

## Running low on credits

When your balance drops below 50 credits, a low-balance warning appears in
the dashboard and Outpulse sends you an email alert.

If your balance reaches 0, monitors continue scanning but no new DMs are
sent. Existing queued actions are held until credits are restored.

<Tip>
  Buy a top-up pack before you hit zero — top-up credits never expire, so there's no risk of buying too early.
</Tip>

## Auto top-up

You can configure auto top-up under **Credits → Settings**. When your
balance drops below your configured threshold, Outpulse automatically charges
your saved payment method for a top-up pack. A 10-minute cooldown prevents
duplicate charges, and you'll get an email notification each time auto top-up
fires.
