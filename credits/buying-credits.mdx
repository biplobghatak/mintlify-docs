title: "Buying credits"
description: "Purchase credit bundles and top up your Outpulse account."
---

You can purchase credits at any time from the **Credits** page in your dashboard.

## Credit bundles

| Bundle | Credits | Price | Cost per credit |
|--------|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Basic** | 300 | $12 | $0.040 |
| **Starter** | 1,200 | $39 | $0.033 |
| **Growth** | 3,000 | $89 | $0.030 |
| **Pro** | 7,000 | $179 | $0.026 |
| **Elite** | 25,000 | $499 | $0.020 |

Larger bundles have a lower cost per credit. Buy a bigger bundle if you're running multiple monitors or using smart auto or full auto modes.

## Purchasing a bundle

1. Go to **Credits** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Buy credits** on the bundle you want
3. You'll be redirected to the checkout page (powered by Dodo Payments)
4. Complete payment
5. Credits are added to your balance immediately after payment confirmation

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Credits purchase page showing the bundle cards and the Buy credits buttons]

## Payment methods

Credit card and other payment methods are supported via Dodo Payments. Accepted methods are shown on the checkout page.

## Purchase history

Your full purchase history is available in the Credits ledger. Each credit purchase appears as a positive entry with the amount, timestamp, and bundle name.

## Receipts

A receipt is emailed to your account email address after every successful purchase.

---

## Running low on credits

When your balance drops below 50 credits, a low-balance warning appears in the dashboard header.

If your balance reaches 0, monitors continue scanning but no new signals are created and no drafts are generated. Existing pending queue items are held until credits are restored.
