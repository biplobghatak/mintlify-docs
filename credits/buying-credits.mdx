title: "Buying credits"
description: "Subscribe to a monthly plan or buy a top-up pack to add credits to your Outpulse account."
---

Outpulse credits come from two sources: your **monthly subscription** (which refills automatically each billing cycle) and **top-up packs** (one-time purchases of lifetime credits).

## Subscription plans

| Plan | Price | Monthly credits | X accounts | Best for |
|------|-------|----------------|------------|----------|
| **Solo** | $49 / mo | 1,000 | 1 | Founders & freelancers |
| **Growth** | $129 / mo | 4,000 | Up to 3 | B2B teams scaling outreach |
| **Agency** | $299 / mo | 12,000 | Up to 10 | Agencies & high-volume teams |

All plans include:
- All signal templates
- Outbound tracker
- Free scanning, scoring, and AI drafting

Subscription credits reset at the start of every billing cycle. Unused subscription credits do not carry over — only top-up rollover credits persist month to month.

## Top-up pack

| Pack | Credits | Price | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|-------|
| **Top-up** | 500 | $20 | Credits never expire — roll over forever |

Top-up credits go into your rollover bucket and are only spent after your monthly subscription credits are exhausted. They're ideal for high-activity months or bridging a gap between billing cycles.

## Subscribing or topping up

1. Go to **Credits** in the left sidebar
2. Choose a subscription plan or click **Buy top-up**
3. You'll be redirected to the checkout page (powered by Dodo Payments)
4. Complete payment
5. Credits are added to your balance immediately after payment confirmation

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Credits purchase page showing the plan cards and top-up option]

## Payment methods

Credit card and other payment methods are supported via Dodo Payments. Accepted methods are shown at checkout.

## Purchase history

Your full purchase history is available in the Credits ledger. Each subscription refill and top-up purchase appears as a positive entry with the amount, timestamp, and type.

## Receipts

A receipt is emailed to your account email address after every successful purchase or subscription renewal.

---

## Running low on credits

When your balance drops below 50 credits, a low-balance warning appears in the dashboard and Outpulse sends you an email alert.

If your balance reaches 0, monitors continue scanning but no new DMs are sent. Existing queued actions are held until credits are restored.

<Tip>
  Buy a top-up pack before you hit zero — top-up credits never expire, so there's no risk of buying too early.
</Tip>
