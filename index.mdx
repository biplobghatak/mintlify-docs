## What is Outpulse?

Outpulse is an AI-powered outreach platform for **X (Twitter)** and **Bluesky**. It watches both networks for posts that match your Ideal Customer Profile, scores each one for purchase intent, and drafts a personalized DM in your authentic writing style — ready to approve or send automatically.

The result: you show up in the right conversations at the right moment, consistently, without spending hours on X every day.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Monitor">
    Set up monitors for keywords, hashtags, competitor accounts, or any search query on X or Bluesky. Outpulse scans every 20 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score">
    Every matching post is scored 1–10 for purchase intent using AI. Only posts above your threshold appear in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draft & send">
    AI drafts a personalized DM in your writing style. You approve it, edit it, or let Outpulse send automatically — your choice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Intent monitoring">
    Create unlimited monitors for keywords, hashtags, competitor accounts, or custom queries. Outpulse deduplicates across a rolling 48-hour window so you never see the same post twice.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI intent scoring">
    Each post is scored 1–10 for purchase intent using Claude. The score reflects how likely the author is to be in a buying conversation. You set the threshold per monitor.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI draft generation">
    Outpulse learns your writing style from sample posts you provide. Drafts match your tone, vocabulary, and typical post length — not generic AI output.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Flexible posting modes">
    Choose manual approval, smart auto (AI decides what's safe to send), or full auto per monitor. You stay in control without micromanaging every DM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="People & CRM">
    Every person you interact with is tracked automatically. Move contacts through stages (Contacted → Lead → Customer) and add notes — no separate CRM needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Workspaces & team">
    Manage multiple X accounts or products in separate workspaces. Invite teammates with scoped roles so everyone sees what they need.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
