## What is Outpulse?

Outpulse is an AI-powered outbound DM platform for **X (Twitter)**. You
point it at the people you want to reach — by scanning an account's
network or a tweet's repliers — filter the pool to your ICP, draft
personalized DMs with AI, and send them in paced batches that respect
daily caps and your posting schedule.

The result: you reach the right people on X without spending hours
copy-pasting messages, and without putting your account at risk.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover">
    Create monitors that scan an account's followers / following or the
    repliers to a specific tweet. Filter the pool by bio keywords and
    follower range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score">
    Every discovered profile gets an AI fit score (1–10) based on your ICP.
    Use the score to decide who's worth messaging.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draft">
    Create a campaign from one or more monitors. AI drafts a personalized
    DM for each lead in your writing style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click Send. Outpulse paces the batch with human-like delays, respects
    your daily caps, and only posts inside your active hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Targeted lead discovery">
    Scan an account's followers / following, or every replier to a specific
    tweet. Apply bio-keyword and follower-range filters before any lead
    enters your pool.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI fit scoring">
    Every discovered profile is scored 1–10 against your ICP. Use the score
    to import only high-fit leads into a campaign.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI DM drafting">
    Outpulse learns your writing style from sample posts. Every draft is
    personalized to the prospect's bio and your campaign context — not
    generic AI output.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bulk campaigns with safe pacing">
    Generate drafts for hundreds of leads in one click, review them, then
    send the batch. Outpulse paces sends with human-like delays and
    enforces daily caps automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Outbound tracker">
    Every DM you send is tracked in a two-pane inbox view with a manual
    pipeline status field. Replies happen natively on X via a one-click
    deep link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Workspaces & team">
    Manage multiple X accounts or products in separate workspaces. Invite
    teammates with scoped roles so everyone sees what they need.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
