Outpulse supports team collaboration within workspaces. You can invite colleagues or clients and assign them a role that limits their access appropriately.

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control. Can manage billing, delete the workspace, and change any setting. Only one owner per workspace. |
| **Admin** | Can do everything except delete the workspace or manage billing. Can invite and remove members. |
| **Member** | Can view and work signals, generate and approve drafts, manage monitors, and update people. Cannot change settings or invite others. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access. Can see signals, people, and analytics but cannot take any actions. |

## Inviting a team member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select a role
5. Click **Send invite**

They'll receive an email with an invitation link. Once they accept and sign in, they'll have access to the workspace with the role you assigned.

---

## Changing a member's role

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click the role dropdown next to their name
4. Select the new role

Changes take effect immediately.

---

## Removing a member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Find the member
3. Click **Remove**

The member loses access to the workspace immediately. Their past actions (approved drafts, notes, etc.) remain in the workspace.

---

## Transferring ownership

Workspace ownership can be transferred to another admin:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click the dropdown next to an admin's name
3. Select **Transfer ownership**

You'll be demoted to admin after confirming the transfer.
