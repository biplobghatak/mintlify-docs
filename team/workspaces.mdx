---
title: "Workspaces"
description: "Use workspaces to manage multiple X accounts, products, or clients from a single Outpulse account."
---

A workspace is a container for everything related to one product or X account: its monitors, leads, campaigns, drafts, ICP config, tone profile, outbound tracker, and team members.

When you sign up, a default workspace is created for you. You can create additional workspaces as your usage grows.

## When to use multiple workspaces

**Multiple products** — If you're building or managing more than one product, create a separate workspace per product. Each workspace has its own ICP and tone profile, so the AI learns independently for each.

**Multiple X accounts** — If you manage X accounts for multiple clients or brands, give each its own workspace. This keeps their monitors, signals, and people completely separate.

**Team isolation** — If different team members should only see their own campaigns, separate workspaces enforce that naturally.

---

## Creating a workspace

1. Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the dashboard
2. Click **New workspace**
3. Enter a name for the workspace
4. Complete the ICP and tone profile setup for this workspace

## Switching workspaces

Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of any page to open the workspace switcher. Select a workspace to switch to it.

All views (campaigns, monitors, leads, outbound tracker, analytics) immediately reflect the selected workspace.

---

## Workspace-scoped settings

The following settings are specific to each workspace and do not carry over to others:

- **ICP configuration** — product, industries, keywords, competitors
- **Tone profile** — writing style, sample posts, formality settings
- **Monitors** — all discovery rules and the leads they've found
- **Campaigns and drafts** — every campaign, its leads, drafts, and sent DMs
- **Outbound tracker** — DM threads and pipeline status
- **Connected X accounts** — which X account this workspace posts from
- **Team members** — who has access to this workspace

Your user account and billing (credits) are shared across all workspaces.

---

## Renaming or deleting a workspace

Go to **Settings** within the workspace:

- **Rename** — updates the workspace name across the UI
- **Delete** — permanently removes the workspace and all its data

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace deletes all monitors, leads, campaigns, drafts, outbound threads, and settings inside it. This cannot be undone. Export any data you need from the **Leads** page first.
</Warning>
