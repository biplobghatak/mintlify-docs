The **Outbound Tracker** (accessible via **Inbox** in the sidebar) is your
post-send workspace. It shows every DM Outpulse has sent on your behalf,
lets you manage pipeline status manually, and gives you a one-click link
to continue the conversation natively on X.

Outpulse is outbound-only — replies aren't polled from X. You handle every
inbound message natively, and use the tracker to remember where each
conversation stands.

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Outbound Tracker showing the two-pane layout: thread list on the left and thread detail on the right]

## Two-pane layout

### Thread list (left pane)

Every DM thread appears as a row showing:

- **Prospect handle and display name**
- **Avatar**
- **Status badge** — current pipeline stage
- **Last outbound** — timestamp of the most recent DM sent
- **Message preview** — first line of the last DM

Click any row to open the thread detail.

### Thread detail (right pane)

Selecting a thread shows:

- **Full message history** — every DM sent to this prospect, in chronological order
- **Status dropdown** — update the pipeline stage manually
- **Private notes field** — internal notes visible only to you (not sent to the prospect)
- **"View Chat on X" button** — deep link to the conversation on X so you can reply natively

If the original send included signal context (the post that triggered it),
that's shown alongside the thread for reference.

---

## Pipeline status

Outpulse does not poll X for inbound replies. You update the pipeline
status manually as the conversation progresses.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `Awaiting reply` | DM sent, no update yet |
| `Replied — hot lead` | Prospect responded positively |
| `Replied — warm` | Prospect responded, interest unclear |
| `Closed — won` | Deal closed or goal achieved |
| `Closed — lost` | Prospect not interested |
| `No response` | Decided not to follow up further |

To update: open the thread, click the **Status** dropdown, choose the new
stage.

---

## Viewing conversations on X

Click **View Chat on X** in the thread detail to open the conversation
directly on X. Replies happen natively there — Outpulse only tracks what
it sends, not what you receive.

---

## Private notes

Each thread has a **Notes** field for internal context — qualification
notes, company details, next steps. Notes are never sent to the prospect
and are only visible to your workspace.

---

## Searching and filtering

Use the search bar at the top of the thread list to filter by handle or
display name. Status filters let you focus on one pipeline stage — for
example, viewing all `Awaiting reply` threads that are due a follow-up.
