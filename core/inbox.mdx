The inbox is where high-intent signals appear after being found and scored by your monitors. It's your daily workspace in Outpulse.

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the full Inbox view showing signal cards with intent scores, filters, and action buttons]

## What a signal shows

Each signal card displays:

- **The original post** — full text, timestamp, and a link to view it on X
- **Author profile** — name, X handle, follower count, bio
- **Intent score** — 1–10 visual bar showing purchase intent strength
- **ICP match reason** — one-sentence explanation of why the AI scored this post for your ICP
- **Action buttons** — Generate reply, Generate DM, Skip, Mark outcome

### Intent score

The score represents how likely the author is to be in a buying conversation relevant to your product.

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 9–10 | Very strong buying signal — high priority |
| 7–8 | Clear intent — worth engaging |
| 5–6 | Moderate interest — below default threshold |
| 1–4 | Weak or indirect signal — discarded by default |

Your monitor's threshold controls which scores make it to the inbox. The default is 7.

## Filters

Use the filters at the top of the inbox to focus on what matters:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Signals waiting for action |
| **Draft generated** | Signal has a draft ready to review |
| **Approved** | Draft approved, in queue or posted |
| **Rejected** | Draft rejected, signal not actioned |
| **Posted** | Reply or DM successfully sent |
| **Skipped** | Signal skipped without engaging |
| **Competitor** | Signals from competitor account monitors |

## Sort options

Sort signals by:
- **Newest** — most recent posts first
- **Intent score** — highest-scoring signals first
- **Follower count** — largest audiences first

## Taking action on a signal

### Generate a reply

Click **Generate reply** to create an AI draft that replies to the post publicly. The draft is written in your tone and references the specific post content.

Costs 1 credit to generate.

### Generate a DM

Click **Generate DM** to create a draft direct message to the post author. DMs are more personal and direct — useful when you want to start a private conversation.

Costs 1 credit to generate.

### Skip

Click **Skip** if the signal isn't relevant or worth engaging with. The signal moves to the Skipped filter and won't reappear.

Skipping is free.

### Mark outcome

If you've already engaged with this person outside of Outpulse (or the conversation led somewhere), use **Mark outcome** to record it without generating a draft.

Options: replied, followed, dmed, lead, customer

## Reviewing drafts in the inbox

When a draft is generated, it appears directly beneath the signal card. You can:

- **Approve** — send the draft to the posting queue
- **Edit and approve** — modify the draft text, then approve
- **Regenerate** — request a new draft (optionally with feedback)
- **Reject** — discard the draft without posting

→ [More on drafts](/core/drafts)

---

## Bulk actions

Select multiple signals using the checkboxes to apply bulk actions:

- **Skip all** — skip all selected signals
- **Generate drafts** — generate drafts for all selected signals at once
- **Approve all** — approve all selected drafts

Bulk draft generation charges 1 credit per signal.

<Tip>
  Sort by intent score and bulk-approve the top 10 signals each morning for an efficient daily workflow.
</Tip>
