Each monitor in Outpulse has its own posting mode. You can run different monitors at different automation levels depending on the context, the stakes, and how much you trust the signal quality.

## The three modes

### Manual

All signals require your involvement. Outpulse surfaces signals in your inbox but doesn't generate drafts or post anything automatically.

Your workflow:
1. Signal appears in inbox
2. You click "Generate reply" or "Generate DM"
3. You review the draft
4. You approve it to send to queue

**Best for:**
- High-stakes monitors (e.g. competitor accounts where tone matters most)
- Early days when you're still dialing in your ICP and tone profile
- Accounts you're being especially careful with

### Smart auto

Outpulse generates drafts automatically for every qualifying signal. The AI then evaluates each draft and decides whether it's high enough quality to post without your review. Drafts that don't meet the bar stay in your inbox for manual approval.

Your workflow:
1. High-quality signals: draft generated and posted automatically
2. Lower-confidence signals: draft appears in inbox for your review

**Best for:**
- Established accounts with a well-tuned tone profile
- High-volume monitors where manual review of every signal isn't practical
- Keyword monitors with specific, high-intent queries

### Full auto

Every qualifying signal gets a draft generated and posted without your input. You only see results in the queue and analytics.

Your workflow:
1. Outpulse handles everything
2. You check the queue and analytics periodically

**Best for:**
- Mature accounts with an extensively trained tone profile
- Very specific, high-precision monitors where false positives are rare
- Situations where speed of response matters (e.g. competitor switching conversations)

<Warning>
  Full auto is powerful but carries the most risk. Start with manual or smart auto for at least 2 weeks before enabling full auto on any monitor. Review your queue closely for the first few days after switching.
</Warning>

## Choosing the right mode

| Scenario | Recommended mode |
|----------|-----------------|
| New account or new monitor | Manual |
| Tone profile set up, ICP tuned | Smart auto |
| 2+ weeks of use, consistent draft quality | Full auto (selective) |
| Competitor mention monitors | Manual or Smart auto |
| Broad keyword monitors | Manual |
| Tight, high-intent keyword queries | Smart auto or Full auto |

---

## Changing the mode

1. Go to **Monitors** in the sidebar
2. Click on the monitor you want to change
3. Update the **Automation mode** field
4. Save

Changes apply to new signals immediately. Existing signals in your inbox are not affected.

---

## Daily caps and safety

Regardless of posting mode, Outpulse enforces daily hard caps on:

- Replies per day
- DMs per day
- Total posts per day

These caps protect your account from being flagged by X's spam detection. Outpulse also applies a random 45–90 second delay between every action to mimic human behavior.

If the daily cap is reached, queued actions are deferred to the next available window within your posting schedule.
