The queue is where approved drafts wait to be posted. It shows you exactly what's scheduled, what's been posted, and what failed — with full control to approve, reject, or retry.

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Queue page showing the three tabs: Pending, Posted, Failed]

## Queue tabs

### Pending

Actions waiting to be posted. Each entry shows:

- The draft text
- The signal it originated from
- The X account it will post from
- The action type (DM only or Reply+DM)
- Estimated post time

From pending, you can:
- **Approve** — confirm the action should post (applies to manual mode)
- **Reject** — remove the action from the queue without posting

### Posted

Successfully sent actions. Each entry shows the posted text, timestamp, and — after 24 hours — engagement metrics: impressions, likes, replies, retweets, bookmarks.

Use this tab to review what went out and evaluate the quality of your outreach.

### Failed

Actions that couldn't be posted due to an error. Common reasons:

| Reason | What to do |
|--------|-----------|
| Rate limit exceeded | Retry after a few hours |
| X API error | Check your connected account is still authorized |
| Daily cap reached | Action will auto-retry the next posting window |
| Account paused | Resume the connected account in Settings |

Click **Retry** to attempt the action again.

## How posting works

When a draft is approved, Outpulse doesn't post it immediately. It goes through the following steps:

1. **Queue** — added to pending
2. **Schedule check** — waits until within your posting hours (e.g. Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm)
3. **Daily cap check** — verifies the daily DM limit for this account hasn't been hit
4. **Similarity check** — ensures the draft isn't too similar to a recently sent message
5. **Human-like delay** — waits before firing to mimic human behaviour
6. **Send** — ping reply fired (best-effort) if Reply+DM mode is selected, then DM sent via X API v2 or Bluesky chat API depending on the monitor's target platform
7. **Charge credits** — 2 credits (DM only) or 5 credits (Reply+DM) deducted on success

If any check fails, the action is either deferred or failed.

---

## Similarity check

Before posting, Outpulse compares the draft against your recent posts. If the content is too similar to something sent recently, the action is held and flagged for review.

This prevents accidental repetition — sending the same message to multiple people, or closely repeating a phrase you've used recently.

---

## Posting schedule

The queue respects your schedule settings. If a draft is approved at 11pm on a Friday and your schedule is Mon–Fri 9am–6pm, it will post on Monday morning.

→ [Configure your posting schedule](/settings/schedule)

## Daily caps

Outpulse enforces per-account daily caps on:

- DMs per account
- Total posts per account

When a cap is reached, remaining queued actions are deferred to the next day. Caps reset at midnight in your configured timezone.

The caps are designed to keep activity within X's acceptable use thresholds and protect your account's health. They are not configurable — they're set based on account age and follower count (warm-up mode applies to new accounts).
