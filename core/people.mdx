The People tab is a lightweight CRM that tracks everyone you interact with through Outpulse. Every time you reply to or DM someone, they're automatically added as a contact. You can then track the relationship as it develops, without leaving Outpulse.

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the People page showing contact cards with stages and last interaction info]

## How contacts are created

Contacts are created automatically when:

- A reply or DM is sent to someone via Outpulse
- You manually mark a signal outcome for someone

You can also add contacts manually if you've engaged outside of Outpulse.

## Contact information

Each contact record shows:

- **X handle and profile** — name, avatar, bio, follower count
- **Stage** — where they are in your pipeline
- **First interaction** — when you first engaged with them
- **Last interaction** — most recent contact date
- **Interaction count** — total number of times you've engaged
- **Last intent score** — the intent score of the last signal from this person
- **Notes** — free-text field for anything you want to remember

---

## Pipeline stages

Move contacts through stages as the relationship progresses:

| Stage | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Contacted** | You've replied or DM'd — no response yet |
| **Responded** | They've replied to your outreach |
| **Warm** | Ongoing conversation, showing interest |
| **Lead** | Expressed clear interest in your product |
| **Customer** | Converted to a paying customer |
| **Not a fit** | Engaged but not the right fit |

Stages are manual — you move contacts forward as the conversation develops.

---

## Updating a stage

Click on any contact to open their record. Click the **Stage** dropdown to update.

You can also bulk-update stages from the People list:
1. Select multiple contacts using the checkboxes
2. Click **Update stage**
3. Choose the new stage

---

## Filtering by stage

Use the stage filter at the top of the People page to view contacts at a specific stage. This makes it easy to focus on your warmest leads or follow up with people who haven't responded.

---

## Adding notes

Open a contact's record and click the notes field to add free-text notes. Useful for:

- Context about their situation ("mentioned they're switching from Salesforce")
- Reminders ("follow up in 2 weeks")
- Key details from the conversation ("budget confirmed, evaluating Q2")

Notes are private to your workspace.

---

## Follow-ups

If someone you replied to hasn't responded after 48 hours, Outpulse automatically flags them for a potential follow-up. You'll see a follow-up prompt on the contact record.

→ [Follow-up system](/core/follow-ups)

---

## Search

Use the search bar on the People page to find contacts by name or X handle.
