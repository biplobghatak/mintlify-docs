[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Analytics page showing the main metrics panels and charts]

## Account metrics

Outpulse takes periodic snapshots of your X account's key metrics so you can track growth over time:

- **Followers** — follower count over time
- **Following** — accounts you follow over time
- **Tweet count** — total tweets posted
- **Listed count** — how many public lists your account appears in

These snapshots are taken automatically at regular intervals. The chart shows the trend over your selected date range.

---

## Activity metrics

### Outreach activity by day

A bar chart showing how many replies and DMs were sent each day. Use this to:

- Spot gaps in your outreach (days with no activity)
- Understand whether your posting schedule is working
- Track consistency over time

### Signals by day

The number of new signals found by your monitors each day. Spikes in signal volume usually indicate:

- A trending conversation in your space
- A new keyword query producing more results
- A competitor update generating discussion

---

## Monitor performance

Each monitor's signal count and engagement rate is shown. Use this to evaluate:

- Which monitors produce the most signals
- Which monitors produce signals that lead to replies/conversations
- Whether a monitor's query needs refinement

## Post engagement

For each post sent through Outpulse, engagement metrics are tracked after 24 hours:

| Metric | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| Impressions | How many people saw the post |
| Likes | Likes on the reply |
| Replies | Replies to your outreach reply |
| Retweets | Retweets of your reply |
| Bookmarks | Bookmarks of your reply |

Use this data to understand which types of replies get traction and refine your approach.

## ROI tracking

The ROI panel compares your Outpulse credit spend to the industry benchmark for Twitter Ads cost-per-engagement.

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Credits spent** | Total credits used in the period |
| **Cost in USD** | Dollar value of credits spent |
| **Engagements generated** | Replies received on your outreach posts |
| **Cost per engagement** | Your cost vs. the Twitter Ads benchmark |

This helps you understand the unit economics of your X outreach vs. paid alternatives.
