A draft is an AI-generated direct message that Outpulse creates for a
specific lead inside a campaign. Each draft is written using your tone
profile, your ICP, the lead's profile data, and any campaign-specific
context you've added.

Drafting happens inside a campaign — not on a per-lead basis from the
**Leads** page. To generate drafts you import leads into a campaign first.

→ [Campaigns](/core/campaigns)

## Generating drafts

From a campaign's detail page:

- **Generate Drafts (n)** in the header bulk-drafts every `pending` lead
  (up to 100 per request — the UI batches if needed)
- **Generate Draft** on any individual lead card drafts that single lead

Each successful draft is **0.25 credits**, deducted from the workspace
owner's balance. Failed generations are free.

The campaign page polls in the background while drafts are running, so
you'll see drafts appear card-by-card as they finish — you don't have to
wait for the entire batch.

## Reviewing a draft

Read the draft carefully before sending. Good drafts:

- Sound like something you'd actually write
- Reference specifics from the lead's bio or context
- Open a conversation rather than make a hard pitch
- Stay an appropriate length (X DMs are plain text up to 10,000 characters)

## Editing a draft

Click **Edit** on any drafted lead's card to open the inline editor. The
editor has a quick-emoji picker and a character counter. Saving updates
the draft in place.

X DMs are plain text: emojis, line breaks, and links work; bold/italic
formatting is stripped on send.

<Tip>
  Even if a draft is 90% right, make the small edits to get it exactly
  right. Editing is free, and it's the easiest way to keep quality high.
</Tip>

## Regenerating a draft

Click **Regenerate** on any drafted lead's card. The draft is replaced
with a fresh attempt that uses the latest workspace ICP, tone profile,
and campaign context.

Regenerating costs the same **0.25 credits** as the initial generation —
each regeneration is a fresh AI call.

<Note>
  If you change your **Campaign Context** mid-batch, existing drafts don't
  update automatically. Use Regenerate on the leads you want to refresh.
</Note>

## Campaign context

The **Context** button at the top of a campaign opens a 2,000-character
guidance field. Use it to override workspace-level framing for this
specific campaign — for example, "skip the demo offer, drop a Loom link
instead." Context is included in every draft for this campaign going
forward.

→ [Campaigns: campaign context](/core/campaigns)

## Edit-and-learn

Outpulse tracks every edit you make to a draft. Over time, the AI uses
these patterns to improve your tone profile — consistently shorter
sentences, removed phrases, different opening styles all feed back in.

After a few weeks of use, you'll find yourself editing less and less.

This happens passively. No configuration required.

---

## Draft quality checklist

Before clicking Send, ask:

- [ ] Does it sound like me?
- [ ] Is it specific to this lead, not generic?
- [ ] Does it invite a response rather than close the conversation?
- [ ] Is it the right length?
- [ ] Would I be proud to send it?

If any answer is no, edit or regenerate.
