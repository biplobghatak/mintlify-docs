The Connected accounts page (**Settings → Connected accounts**) is where you manage which X and Bluesky accounts Outpulse can post from.

Each connected account is scoped to the current workspace. The same account can be connected in multiple workspaces independently.

## Adding an X account

1. Go to **Settings → Connected accounts**
2. Click **Connect new account** in the X card
3. You'll be redirected to X's OAuth page — authorize Outpulse
4. You'll be redirected back and the account will appear in the list

## Adding a Bluesky account

Bluesky uses an **app password** instead of OAuth. Generate one at [bsky.app/settings/app-passwords](https://bsky.app/settings/app-passwords) with **direct messages** access enabled, then:

1. Go to **Settings → Connected accounts**
2. In the **Bluesky** card, click **Connect Bluesky**
3. Enter your handle (e.g. `yourname.bsky.social`) and paste the app password
4. Click **Connect**

→ [Full Bluesky connection guide](/getting-started/connect-bluesky-account)

## Account status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Connected and posting normally |
| **Paused** | Connected but posting stopped |
| **Error** | Authorization issue — needs reconnection |

If an account shows an error status, disconnect it and reconnect. The most common cause is an expired or revoked token — for X that means re-authorizing OAuth, for Bluesky that means generating a fresh app password.

## Pausing an account

Pausing stops all posting from the account without disconnecting it. Monitors continue scanning and scoring — no signals are lost. Drafts accumulate in the queue as pending but won't be posted until you resume.

To pause: click **Pause** next to the account.

## Resuming an account

Click **Resume** to restart posting. Queued actions will begin processing on the next posting window.

## Disconnecting an account

Disconnecting removes the account from Outpulse and deletes its stored tokens. Any monitors tied to this account will be paused automatically.

<Warning>
  Disconnecting does not revoke authorization on the platform side.

  - **X:** go to **X Settings → Security and account access → Connected apps** and remove Outpulse there.
  - **Bluesky:** go to [bsky.app/settings/app-passwords](https://bsky.app/settings/app-passwords) and delete the Outpulse app password.
</Warning>

## Your profile

Your user profile (**Settings → Profile**) controls your display name, email address, and profile picture shown within Outpulse. This is separate from your connected X account profile.

To update your email, enter the new address and verify it via the confirmation link sent to the new email.

To change your password, click **Change password** and follow the prompts.
