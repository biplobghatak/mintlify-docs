Outpulse can send you real-time notifications when a high-fit lead is discovered, so you can act on hot prospects without watching the leads page constantly.

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to configure.

## Slack webhook

Connect a Slack webhook to receive signal alerts directly in a Slack channel.

### Setting up a Slack webhook

1. In Slack, go to **Your workspace → Settings → Integrations → Incoming Webhooks**
2. Click **Add new webhook to workspace**
3. Select the channel you want to receive alerts in
4. Copy the webhook URL
5. Paste it into **Settings → Notifications → Slack webhook URL** in Outpulse
6. Click **Save**

[IMAGE: Screenshot of the Notifications settings page with the Slack webhook URL field]

To test the connection, click **Send test notification** after saving.

### Webhook alert format

Each alert includes:

- The monitor that surfaced the lead
- The author's X handle and follower count
- The AI fit score and the reason
- A direct link to the lead in Outpulse

### Removing a webhook

Clear the webhook URL field and save to stop Slack notifications.

---

## Alert threshold

The alert threshold controls the minimum AI fit score required to trigger a notification. Lower-scored leads are still saved on the Leads page but won't send an alert.

**Default: 8**

Raise this if you're getting too many Slack notifications. Lower it if you want to be alerted about more leads.

<Tip>
  Set your alert threshold 1-2 points above your campaign import threshold.
  Only the strongest fits ping you immediately — you can browse the rest
  on the Leads page at your convenience.
</Tip>

## Email notifications

Email digests are sent weekly with a summary of:

- Leads found
- Drafts generated and DMs sent
- Outbound tracker activity
- Credit usage

You can't configure the email digest frequency — it's weekly. To stop receiving it, unsubscribe via the link at the bottom of the email.
